# TiXL Discord Engagement Program (TIXL-092)

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive Discord engagement program establishes TiXL as a vibrant, collaborative community for real-time motion graphics creators, developers, and enthusiasts. The program focuses on fostering knowledge sharing, technical excellence, and mutual support while maintaining the architectural rigor and professional standards that define the TiXL project.

## üéØ Program Objectives

- **Knowledge Sharing**: Create an environment where users and developers exchange insights, techniques, and best practices
- **Technical Excellence**: Promote the high-quality standards reflected in TiXL's architectural governance
- **Community Growth**: Attract and retain engaged members through valuable content and interactions
- **Developer Support**: Provide comprehensive support for contributors while maintaining code quality
- **Innovation**: Foster creative experimentation with real-time motion graphics

#### **Mentors** (@Mentor)

- **Access**: New member channels, help desk coordination
- **Requirements**: Patient, knowledgeable, available for regular assistance
- **Benefits**: Priority access to beta features, exclusive community events

#### **Contributors** (@Contributor)
- **Responsibilities**: Share knowledge, participate in discussions, create helpful content
- **Access**: Member-only channels, early feature previews
- **Requirements**: Active participation (weekly engagement), constructive contributions

### Tier 4: Community Members

#### **Members** (@Member)
- **Responsibilities**: Participate in discussions, follow community guidelines
- **Access**: General channels, voting on community decisions
- **Requirements**: Verified Discord account, adherence to code of conduct

## üõ°Ô∏è Moderation Guidelines

### **Community Standards**

#### **Core Principles**
1. **Respect**: Treat all members with dignity and professionalism
2. **Constructiveness**: Provide helpful, actionable feedback
3. **Inclusivity**: Welcome diverse perspectives and skill levels
4. **Excellence**: Maintain TiXL's high technical and creative standards
5. **Collaboration**: Foster knowledge sharing and mutual support

#### **Prohibited Behaviors**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Spamming, excessive self-promotion, or off-topic content
- Sharing pirated software, assets, or illegal content
- Deliberately misleading or harmful technical advice
- Violating others' intellectual property rights

### **Moderation Tiers**

#### **Tier 1: Warning (Green)**
**Trigger**: Minor guideline violations, repeated off-topic posts
**Response**: Private message reminder, guidance on proper behavior
**Documentation**: Internal tracking, no public record

#### **Tier 2: Timeout (Yellow)**
**Trigger**: Continued minor violations, moderate spam, inappropriate content
**Response**: 24-72 hour timeout, detailed explanation required for return
**Documentation**: Public warning issued, behavior monitoring

#### **Tier 3: Temporary Ban (Orange)**
**Trigger**: Serious violations, harassment, harmful misinformation
**Response**: 7-30 day ban, community manager review required
**Documentation**: Full incident report, community notification

#### **Tier 4: Permanent Ban (Red)**
**Trigger**: Extreme violations, illegal activities, sustained harmful behavior
**Response**: Immediate permanent ban, detailed justification
**Documentation**: Comprehensive incident report, maintainer notification

### **Moderation Process**

#### **Response Time Targets**
- **Critical Issues**: Immediate response (within 5 minutes)
- **Urgent Issues**: Within 1 hour
- **Standard Issues**: Within 4 hours
- **Review Items**: Within 24 hours

#### **Decision Framework**
1. **Assess**: Evaluate severity and context
2. **Document**: Record incident details and reasoning
3. **Respond**: Apply appropriate tier response
4. **Follow-up**: Monitor situation and member behavior
5. **Review**: Team discussion for learning and improvement

### **Escalation Path**
1. **Community Ambassadors**: Handle routine moderation
2. **Community Managers**: Manage complex situations and appeals
3. **Project Maintainers**: Final authority on serious violations

## üìà Community Health Metrics

### **Engagement Metrics**

#### **Daily Active Users (DAU)**
- **Target**: 15% of total membership
- **Measurement**: Unique users posting or reacting per day
- **Strategy**: Daily content prompts, event notifications
- **Alert Threshold**: <10% for 3 consecutive days

#### **Weekly Active Users (WAU)**
- **Target**: 40% of total membership
- **Measurement**: Unique users active per week
- **Strategy**: Weekly challenges, community spotlights
- **Alert Threshold**: <30% for 2 consecutive weeks

#### **Message Velocity**
- **Target**: 50+ messages per day across all channels
- **Quality Focus**: Meaningful contributions over volume
- **Monitoring**: Spikes (potential issues) and drops (engagement problems)
- **Response**: Content adjustments, event scheduling

#### **New Member Retention**
- **30-Day Retention**: >60% of new members remain active
- **90-Day Retention**: >40% become regular contributors
- **Measurement**: Activity tracking of new joiners
- **Intervention**: Enhanced onboarding for low retention periods

### **Quality Metrics**

#### **Response Time**
- **Newbie Questions**: <2 hours average response time
- **Technical Help**: <4 hours for non-urgent issues
- **Bug Reports**: <24 hours initial assessment
- **Moderation**: <1 hour for reported issues

#### **Content Quality**
- **Tutorial Quality**: Community feedback scores >4.0/5.0
- **Technical Accuracy**: Peer review validation for complex topics
- **Helpfulness Ratings**: Community appreciation for support responses
- **Constructive Feedback**: Ratio of positive to negative feedback

#### **Member Satisfaction**
- **Monthly Surveys**: Community health and satisfaction measurement
- **Net Promoter Score**: Community recommendation likelihood
- **Exit Interviews**: Understanding member departure reasons
- **Suggestion Implementation**: Tracking community-driven improvements

### **Growth Metrics**

#### **Member Acquisition**
- **New Member Rate**: 5-10 new members per week
- **Source Tracking**: Discord discovery, social media, word-of-mouth
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitor to member conversion tracking
- **Quality Focus**: Engaging, long-term members over raw numbers

#### **Role Progression**
- **Newcomer to Member**: >50% conversion within 30 days
- **Member to Contributor**: >25% progression within 90 days
- **Contributor to Mentor**: >10% achievement within 6 months
- **Ambassador Pipeline**: Consistent progression to leadership roles

### **Health Monitoring Tools**

#### **Discord Analytics**
- **Member Activity Tracking**: Daily/weekly active user monitoring
- **Channel Usage Analysis**: Most/least active channel identification
- **Reaction Patterns**: Engagement quality assessment
- **Message Sentiment**: Community mood and satisfaction tracking

#### **Community Surveys**
- **Monthly Pulse Checks**: Short 5-question community health surveys
- **Quarterly Deep Dives**: Comprehensive community satisfaction analysis
- **Annual Community Review**: Strategic planning and goal setting
- **Event Feedback**: Post-event satisfaction and improvement suggestions

#### **Automated Monitoring**
- **Engagement Alerts**: Automatic notifications for low activity periods
- **Content Moderation**: AI-assisted inappropriate content detection
- **Spam Prevention**: Automated spam detection and removal
- **Member Journey Tracking**: Onboarding progress monitoring

### **Intervention Strategies**

#### **Low Engagement Response**
1. **Immediate**: Increase event frequency and community challenges
2. **Short-term**: Implement content theme weeks and guest features
3. **Medium-term**: Survey members for feedback and preferences
4. **Long-term**: Strategic planning for community growth and engagement

#### **Quality Decline Response**
1. **Immediate**: Increased moderator presence and guidance
2. **Short-term**: Content quality workshops and training sessions
3. **Medium-term**: Community guidelines review and reinforcement
4. **Long-term**: Recognition system enhancement and incentive programs

#### **Growth Plateau Response**
1. **Immediate**: Targeted outreach to inactive members
2. **Short-term**: Referral programs and community showcases
3. **Medium-term**: Strategic partnerships and cross-community collaboration
4. **Long-term**: Platform expansion and enhanced onboarding
