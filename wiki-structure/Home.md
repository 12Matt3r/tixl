## What is TiXL?

TiXL is an open-source platform for creating real-time motion graphics that features:

- **Real-time rendering** with DirectX 12
- **Graph-based procedural** content generation
- **Linear keyframe animation** system
- **Audio-reactive** visual creation
- **Plugin-based operator** system
- **Modern C#** development with .NET 9.0+
