# Community Recognition Program

## Overview

The TiXL Community Recognition Program celebrates and acknowledges the outstanding contributions of individuals who help make TiXL better for everyone. We believe in recognizing not just code contributions, but all forms of participation that strengthen our community.

## Recognition Philosophy

### Core Values

- **Inclusivity**: Recognition is available to all types of contributions
- **Diversity**: We celebrate different kinds of contributions and contributors
- **Growth**: Recognition encourages continued learning and contribution
- **Community**: We build each other up and celebrate collective success
- **Transparency**: Recognition criteria are clear and transparent
- **Ongoing**: Recognition is not just one-time but ongoing

### Recognition Principles

1. **Multiple Paths**: There are many ways to be recognized
2. **Peer-Nominated**: Community members can nominate others
3. **Maintainers Acknowledge**: Core team validates and publicizes recognition
4. **No Competition**: Recognition is collaborative, not competitive
5. **Cultural Sensitivity**: Recognition considers cultural and personal contexts
6. **Inclusive Language**: All recognition uses inclusive and respectful language

## Types of Recognition

### Automatic Recognition

These recognitions are automatically granted based on contribution metrics:

#### First Contribution Badge
**Criteria:**
- First accepted pull request
- First merged documentation contribution
- First successful issue report

**Benefits:**
- "First Contributor" Discord badge
- Mention in community welcome posts
- Invitation to newcomer mentoring channel

#### Regular Contributor
**Criteria:**
- 5+ meaningful contributions over 6 months
- Consistent positive engagement in community
- Quality code reviews or documentation

**Benefits:**
- "Regular Contributor" Discord role
- Priority in community polls and feedback requests
- Access to contributor-only discussions

#### Long-term Contributor
**Criteria:**
- 12+ months of consistent contribution
- 20+ meaningful contributions
- Positive community reputation

**Benefits:**
- "Veteran Contributor" Discord role
- Consideration for core maintainer nomination
- Speaking opportunities at community events

#### Release Contributor
**Criteria:**
- Contribution included in official release
- Any code, documentation, or content in release
- Active participation in release process

**Benefits:**
- Featured in release notes
- "Release Contributor" badge
- Special recognition in release announcement

### Peer Nomination Recognition

Community members can nominate others for:

#### Community Helper
**Nomination Criteria:**
- Consistently helpful in support channels
- Welcoming to newcomers
- Provides quality answers and guidance
- Goes above and beyond to help others

**Nomination Process:**
1. Community member submits nomination via form
2. Maintainers verify contribution claims
3. Community discussion period (1 week)
4. Final decision by core maintainer team
5. Public announcement and celebration

**Benefits:**
- "Community Helper" Discord role
- Monthly community spotlight
- Badge on GitHub profile
- Small token of appreciation (when available)

#### Documentation Champion
**Nomination Criteria:**
- Exceptional documentation contributions
- Clear, helpful writing style
- Significant improvements to guides and tutorials
- Mentoring others in documentation

**Benefits:**
- "Documentation Champion" Discord role
- Special mention in documentation repository
- Opportunity to lead documentation initiatives
- Annual appreciation gift (when budget allows)

#### Creative Excellence
**Nomination Criteria:**
- Outstanding creative work using TiXL
- Innovative use of operators and features
- High-quality tutorials or showcases
- Inspiring others through creative work

**Benefits:**
- "Creative Excellence" Discord role
- Featured in monthly creative showcase
- Potential collaboration opportunities
- Invitation to creative advisory group

#### Technical Innovation
**Nomination Criteria:**
- Innovative technical solutions or features
- Performance improvements or optimizations
- Novel operator or plugin development
- Advanced technical contributions

**Benefits:**
- "Technical Innovation" Discord role
- Technical spotlight in community updates
- Involvement in technical roadmap discussions
- Mentorship opportunities with developers

#### Community Ambassador
**Nomination Criteria:**
- Promotes TiXL positively in external communities
- Organizes or participates in community events
- Welcomes and mentors newcomers
- Represents TiXL values well

**Benefits:**
- "Community Ambassador" Discord role
- Speaking opportunities at events
- Collaboration with community team
- Support for community initiatives

#### Inclusive Community Builder
**Nomination Criteria:**
- Actively promotes inclusivity and diversity
- Creates welcoming environment for all
- Addresses accessibility needs
- Educates others on inclusive practices

**Benefits:**
- "Inclusive Community Builder" Discord role
- Leadership in community inclusivity initiatives
- Special projects focused on accessibility
- Recognition in diversity and inclusion reports

## Nomination and Selection Process

### Nomination Procedures

#### Open Nomination Period
- Year-round nomination acceptance
- Clear nomination form and guidelines
- Self-nomination encouraged
- Peer nomination process

#### Nomination Review
- Initial review within 1 week
- Community feedback period (2 weeks)
- Maintainer decision meeting
- Appeals process for rejected nominations

#### Selection Criteria
- Consistency of positive contribution
- Community impact and influence
- Quality of work and collaboration
- Alignment with community values

### Selection Committee

#### Committee Composition
- 3-5 core maintainers
- 2-3 community representatives
- Rotating membership (annual terms)
- Diverse representation

#### Decision Process
- Committee meetings monthly
- Consensus-based decisions
- Documentation of rationale
- Transparent communication of decisions
