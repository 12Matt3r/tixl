# TIXL-097 Content Cadence Policy

## Executive Summary

This document establishes a comprehensive content creation and publication cadence system for TiXL, designed to ensure consistent, high-quality content delivery that educates, engages, and grows our community. The system encompasses blog posts, tutorials, releases, community spotlights, and educational materials with specific publication schedules, themes, and engagement strategies.

## Content Strategy Overview

### Vision
Create a thriving ecosystem of educational and engaging content that positions TiXL as the leading solution for audio-visual scheduling, node editor development, and real-time audio processing.

### Mission
Deliver consistent, valuable content that:
- Educates developers about TiXL capabilities
- Showcases real-world applications and use cases
- Builds and strengthens the community
- Drives adoption and innovation
- Establishes thought leadership in the domain

### Core Principles
- **Consistency**: Regular publication schedule across all content types
- **Quality**: High standards for technical accuracy and readability
- **Value**: Every piece should provide actionable insights
- **Community-First**: Content that serves community needs and feedback
- **Innovation**: Stay current with industry trends and technologies

## Content Types and Cadence

### 1. Blog Posts

#### Technical Deep Dives
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly (every 2 weeks)
- **Length**: 1,500-3,000 words
- **Focus**: Advanced technical topics, architecture insights, performance optimization
- **Audience**: Experienced developers, system architects

#### Feature Spotlights
- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Length**: 800-1,200 words
- **Focus**: New features, improvements, API changes
- **Audience**: Existing users, developers evaluating TiXL

#### Industry Insights
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Length**: 1,000-2,000 words
- **Focus**: Trends, comparisons, best practices
- **Audience**: Technical leaders, decision makers

### 2. Tutorials

#### Getting Started Guides
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly
- **Length**: 2,000-4,000 words
- **Focus**: Step-by-step guides for new users
- **Audience**: Beginners, newcomers to TiXL

#### Advanced Use Cases
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Length**: 3,000-5,000 words
- **Focus**: Complex scenarios, integration patterns
- **Audience**: Experienced developers

#### Video Tutorials
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Length**: 10-30 minutes
- **Focus**: Visual demonstrations, walkthroughs
- **Audience**: All levels, visual learners

### 3. Release Notes

#### Major Releases
- **Frequency**: As needed (typically quarterly)
- **Length**: 1,000-2,000 words
- **Focus**: New features, breaking changes, migration guides
- **Audience**: All users

#### Minor Updates
- **Frequency**: As needed (monthly to quarterly)
- **Length**: 500-800 words
- **Focus**: Bug fixes, small improvements
- **Audience**: Active users

#### Security Updates
- **Frequency**: As needed
- **Length**: 300-500 words
- **Focus**: Security patches, urgent updates
- **Audience**: All users, system administrators

### 4. Community Spotlights

#### Developer Showcases
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly
- **Length**: 800-1,200 words
- **Focus**: Community projects, use cases, success stories
- **Audience**: Community members, potential users

#### Team Interviews
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Length**: 1,000-1,500 words
- **Focus**: Team member insights, development stories
- **Audience**: Community, potential contributors

#### Community Contributions
- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Length**: 300-500 words
- **Focus**: Contributor highlights, community achievements
- **Audience**: Contributors, community members

### 5. Educational Materials

#### White Papers
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Length**: 5,000-8,000 words
- **Focus**: In-depth research, methodology, best practices
- **Audience**: Technical leaders, researchers

#### Case Studies
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Length**: 2,000-3,000 words
- **Focus**: Real-world implementations, results
- **Audience**: Decision makers, potential users

#### Documentation Updates
- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Length**: Variable
- **Focus**: API documentation, guides, references
- **Audience**: All users

## Publication Schedules

### Weekly Schedule
- **Monday**: Feature Spotlight / Community Contribution
- **Wednesday**: Getting Started Tutorial / Blog Post
- **Friday**: Documentation Update / Quick Tip

### Bi-weekly Schedule (Alternating)
- **Week 1**: Technical Deep Dive + Getting Started Tutorial
- **Week 2**: Feature Spotlight + Developer Showcase

### Monthly Schedule
- **Week 1**: Industry Insights / Case Study
- **Week 2**: Team Interview / Advanced Use Case
- **Week 3**: White Paper / Research Analysis
- **Week 4**: Monthly Summary / Community Roundup

### Quarterly Schedule
- **Q1**: Major Release + Security White Paper
- **Q2**: Community Survey + Best Practices Guide
- **Q3**: Architecture Deep Dive + Performance Study
- **Q4**: Year in Review + Future Roadmap

## Content Themes and Series

### Quarterly Themes
- **Q1**: Foundation & Growth - Focus on onboarding and basic concepts
- **Q2**: Performance & Scale - Emphasis on optimization and enterprise use
- **Q3**: Innovation & Integration - Showcase advanced features and partnerships
- **Q4**: Community & Future - Highlight community contributions and future plans

### Recurring Series
1. **"TiXL Tuesday Tutorials"** - Weekly educational content
2. **"Feature Friday"** - Weekly feature spotlights
3. **"Developer Spotlight"** - Bi-weekly community showcases
4. **"Performance Pulse"** - Monthly performance insights
5. **"Architecture Insights"** - Quarterly deep dives
6. **"Community Corner"** - Weekly community updates

### Annual Campaigns
- **TiXLCon Special Coverage** - Conference content and recordings
- **Release Celebration Series** - Major release marketing
- **Developer Appreciation Month** - Community recognition
- **Year-End State of TiXL** - Annual review and roadmap

## Audience Targeting

### Primary Audiences

#### Developers (40% of content)
- **Needs**: Technical documentation, tutorials, best practices
- **Content Preferences**: Code examples, technical deep dives, troubleshooting
- **Engagement**: GitHub discussions, technical forums, Stack Overflow

#### System Architects (25% of content)
- **Needs**: Architecture insights, integration guidance, performance data
- **Content Preferences**: White papers, case studies, comparison guides
- **Engagement**: Technical blogs, conferences, professional networks

#### Decision Makers (20% of content)
- **Needs**: Business value, ROI data, implementation guidance
- **Content Preferences**: Executive summaries, case studies, market analysis
- **Engagement**: LinkedIn, industry publications, webinars

#### Community Contributors (15% of content)
- **Needs**: Contribution guidelines, recognition, community updates
- **Content Preferences**: Community spotlights, contribution tutorials, news
- **Engagement**: Discord, GitHub, community forums

### Content Personalization
- **Developer Track**: Heavy technical content with code examples
- **Business Track**: Strategic content with ROI focus
- **Community Track**: Engagement-focused content with recognition
- **Learning Track**: Educational progression from basics to advanced

## Quality Standards

### Content Quality Criteria
1. **Technical Accuracy**: All code examples and technical information verified
2. **Clarity**: Clear, concise writing with proper structure
3. **Actionability**: Every piece provides actionable insights or steps
4. **Completeness**: Full coverage of topic with supporting resources
5. **Freshness**: Current information with regular updates

### Review Process
1. **Technical Review**: Subject matter expert verification
2. **Editorial Review**: Writing quality and clarity check
3. **Community Review**: Beta reader feedback for key content
4. **Final Approval**: Content lead final sign-off

### Style Guide Requirements
- **Voice**: Professional yet approachable
- **Tone**: Educational and helpful
- **Format**: Consistent structure with clear headings
- **Visuals**: Include diagrams, screenshots, and code examples
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance

- **SEO Optimization Gate**: Keyword and structure validation

## Review and Update Process

### Regular Reviews
- **Monthly**: Content performance review and optimization
- **Quarterly**: Strategy review and theme adjustment
- **Annually**: Comprehensive policy review and update
- **Ad-hoc**: Response to major events or community feedback

### Continuous Improvement
- **A/B Testing**: Regular testing of formats and topics
