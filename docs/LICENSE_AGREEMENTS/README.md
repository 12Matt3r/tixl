# TiXL License Agreement Templates

This directory contains standardized licensing templates for TiXL, designed to provide clear, comprehensive legal frameworks for different use cases.

## Template Overview

### 1. Commercial License Template
**File:** `commercial-license-template.md`
**Purpose:** For-profit organizations and commercial enterprises
**Key Features:**
- Comprehensive commercial usage rights
- Multiple licensing tiers (Community, Professional, Enterprise, OEM)
- Professional services and support packages
- Export control compliance
- Intellectual property protection

### 2. Educational License Template
**File:** `educational-license-template.md`
**Purpose:** Academic institutions, educational organizations, students, and researchers
**Key Features:**
- Free licensing for educational use
- Research and publication rights
- Curriculum integration support
- Student and faculty programs
- International educational compliance

### 3. Enterprise License Template
**File:** `enterprise-license-template.md`
**Purpose:** Large corporations and enterprise organizations
**Key Features:**
- Multiple enterprise deployment models
- Comprehensive support and service levels
- Strategic partnership opportunities
- Custom development and integration
- SLA guarantees and compliance features

### 4. Individual Contributor License Agreement (ICLA)
**File:** `individual-contributor-icla-template.md`
**Purpose:** Individual contributors to the TiXL project
**Key Features:**
- Copyright and patent license grants
- Legal protection for contributors
- Community rights and recognition
- Moral rights waivers
- Dispute resolution procedures

### 5. Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)
**File:** `corporate-contributor-ccla-template.md`
**Purpose:** Corporate organizations and their employees/contractors
**Key Features:**
- Corporate authority and governance
- Enhanced commercial rights
- Patent portfolio management
- Strategic partnership framework
- Compliance and risk management

## Usage Guidelines

### Template Selection
Choose the appropriate template based on:

1. **Use Case Identification**
   - Commercial product development → Commercial License
   - Educational institution use → Educational License
   - Large enterprise deployment → Enterprise License
   - Individual code contribution → ICLA
   - Corporate code contribution → CCLA

2. **Legal Requirements**
   - Review local laws and regulations
   - Consider international trade implications
   - Assess intellectual property risks
   - Evaluate liability and insurance needs

3. **Business Considerations**
   - Revenue model alignment
   - Support and service requirements
   - Strategic partnership opportunities
   - Compliance and audit needs

### Customization Process

#### Legal Review Required
- All templates require legal review before use
- Consult with qualified legal counsel
- Adapt terms to specific jurisdictions
- Consider industry-specific requirements

#### Customization Steps
1. **Identify Required Changes**
   - Determine applicable terms modifications
   - Identify missing provisions
   - Assess liability and warranty terms

2. **Legal Counsel Review**
   - Engage qualified legal counsel
   - Provide full template and requirements
   - Review all proposed changes
   - Ensure compliance with local law

3. **Stakeholder Approval**
   - Obtain internal approvals
   - Review with business stakeholders
   - Confirm financial and operational terms
   - Secure executive approval

4. **Final Documentation**
   - Execute properly executed agreements
   - Maintain signed copies
   - Implement compliance procedures
   - Monitor ongoing compliance
