## Contributor License Agreements

### Individual Contributor License Agreement (ICLA)

While TiXL currently operates under an implied license, we may implement a formal ICLA for:
- **Legal Clarity**: Explicit permission grants
- **Patent Protection**: Contributor patent licenses
- **Representation**: Authority to contribute on behalf of employers

**Key Terms:**
- Contributor retains copyright ownership
- Project receives perpetual, irrevocable license
- Contributor grants patent license for contributions
- Contributor represents authority to contribute
- Standard Apache-style ICLA framework

### Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)

For contributions from employees or contractors:
- Corporate entity grants broader permissions
- Ensures organization approves contributions
- Covers patent rights and representations
- Required for significant organizational contributions

### Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO)

To ensure contribution provenance:
- All commits must be signed with DCO
- Contributors certify they have right to submit code
- Simple Linux kernel-style DCO process
- Automated checks in CI/CD pipeline

## Trademark Protection

### TiXL Trademark

**Trademark Registration:**
- "TiXL" is a registered trademark
- Protects project identity and reputation
- Prevents confusion with other software

**Usage Guidelines:**
- Can use "TiXL" to refer to the software
- Cannot use in company or product names without permission
- Must use appropriate trademark notices
- Cannot imply endorsement without permission

**Permitted Uses:**
- "Built with TiXL"
- "Compatible with TiXL"
- "Uses TiXL technology"
- "TiXL community"

**Prohibited Uses:**
- "TiXL Pro" or "TiXL Enterprise"
- Using logo without permission
- Implying official endorsement
- Misleading product names

### Domain Names

**Official Domains:**
- tixl.app (primary)
- tixl-project.org
- tixl-graphics.com

**Community Guidelines:**
- Avoid domains that could cause confusion
- Do not register conflicting domains
- Report domain abuse to maintainers

**Our Patents:**

- Users must assess patent risks of use

### Patent Licensing

**Implicit Patent License:**
- MIT license includes patent license

**Defensive Patent Strategy:**
