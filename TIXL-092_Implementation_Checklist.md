## Emergency Procedures

### Moderation Escalation
- [ ] **Tier 1 Issues** (warnings): Handle within 1 hour
- [ ] **Tier 2 Issues** (timeouts): Handle within 30 minutes
- [ ] **Tier 3 Issues** (temp bans): Handle within 15 minutes
- [ ] **Tier 4 Issues** (permabans): Handle immediately

### Community Crisis Response
- [ ] **Low Engagement Crisis**: Activate emergency content calendar
- [ ] **Quality Decline**: Increase moderator presence and guidance
- [ ] **Major Community Issue**: Emergency community meeting within 24 hours
- [ ] **Safety/Security Issue**: Immediate response and documentation

### Communication Protocols
- [ ] **Member Issues**: Private message first, escalate if needed
- [ ] **Moderation Decisions**: Document reasons, provide appeal process
- [ ] **Community Updates**: Use #announcements for important information
- [ ] **Emergency Communications**: All-staff alert system activation

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Engagement Targets
- [ ] **Daily Active Users**: 15% of total membership
- [ ] **Weekly Active Users**: 40% of total membership
- [ ] **Message Velocity**: 50+ messages per day average
- [ ] **New Member Retention**: 60% at 30 days, 40% at 90 days

### Quality Targets
- [ ] **Response Time**: <2 hours for newbie questions, <4 hours for technical help
- [ ] **Content Quality**: Tutorial ratings >4.0/5.0, constructive feedback ratio >80%
- [ ] **Member Satisfaction**: Net Promoter Score >50, monthly survey satisfaction >4.0/5.0

### Growth Targets
- [ ] **New Member Acquisition**: 5-10 new members per week
- [ ] **Role Progression**: >50% newcomer→member, >25% member→contributor conversion
- [ ] **Community Events**: Average 10+ attendees per weekly event
